Plan Your Visit to Tall Pines ATV Park

Gear up for a thrilling off-roading adventure at Tall Pines ATV Park, the ultimate destination for outdoor enthusiasts. To ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience for the whole family, it's essential to plan your visit in advance. Here are some essential details to keep in mind before embarking on your ultimate off-road adventure:

  • Park Hours: Tall Pines ATV Park is open for riding all year round, weather permitting. During the summer months, the park is open from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. from Monday to Thursday, and from 9 a.m. to sunset on Friday, Saturday, and Sunday. In the off-season, hours vary, so be sure to check the park's website for updated information.
  • Admission Fees: Admission prices vary depending on the age of the riders and the type of vehicle used. Children under five enter for free, while adults pay a fee of $20. For ATV and dirt bike riders, the price ranges from $30 to $50.
  • Safety Guidelines: To ensure the safety of all visitors, Tall Pines ATV Park has established a set of rules and guidelines that all riders must follow. This includes wearing appropriate safety gear, following speed limits, and staying on designated trails.
  • Other Information: Be sure to check the park's website for additional details, such as camping information, events and festivals, and contact details to get in touch with the park's staff.
