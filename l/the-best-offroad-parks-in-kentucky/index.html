The Best Offroad Parks in Kentucky

01/28/2024

Kentucky is home to several popular offroad parks that offer thrilling adventures for offroading enthusiasts. Whether you're an experienced offroader or a beginner looking to explore the trails, these parks cater to all skill levels.

Key Takeaways:

  • Kentucky offers a variety of offroad parks for enthusiasts to enjoy.
  • These parks provide trails suitable for all skill levels.
  • Parks such as Leatherwood Off Road Park and Wildcat Offroad Park offer extensive trail networks.
  • Hollerwood Park provides a family-friendly atmosphere in a scenic mountain setting.
  • Black Mountain Off-Road Adventure Area offers challenging trails with breathtaking views of the Appalachian Mountains.
  • The Land Between the Lakes National Recreation Area is a vast natural expanse that also offers offroading opportunities.

Leatherwood Off Road Park

Located in Evarts, Kentucky, Leatherwood Off Road Park is a premier destination for offroad enthusiasts. With its vast 8,000 acres of land and over 150 miles of thrilling trails, this offroad park offers endless adventures for adrenaline junkies.

Open year-round, Leatherwood Off Road Park welcomes all types of offroad vehicles, including ATVs, side-by-sides, dirt bikes, trucks, Jeeps, and buggies. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced offroader, the park caters to all skill levels, ensuring there's something for everyone.

The trails at Leatherwood Off Road Park vary in difficulty, allowing riders to choose the level of challenge that suits them best. The routes are clearly marked, providing guidance and ensuring a safe and enjoyable offroading experience.

Wildcat Offroad Park

Wildcat Offroad Park is located in East Bernstadt, Kentucky. Spanning over 2,000 acres, it offers offroad enthusiasts of all skill levels more than 100 miles of thrilling trails to explore. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced offroader, this park provides a perfect playground for your offroad adventures.

The park boasts a full-hook-up campground with RV sites and cozy cabins, allowing visitors to extend their stay and fully immerse themselves in the offroading experience. So, after a day of conquering the trails, you can relax and unwind in the comfort of your own accommodation.

Wildcat Offroad Park is also known for hosting various events throughout the year, adding an extra level of excitement to your offroading journey. From organized races to trail rides, there's always something happening at the park. Be sure to check their website regularly for updates on upcoming activities and events.

Hollerwood Park

Hollerwood Park is an offroad park located in Stanton, Kentucky. Owned and operated by a local family, this park offers a welcoming atmosphere surrounded by the beauty of the mountains and a wooded setting. With over 43,000 acres of reclaimed coal lands, Hollerwood Park provides offroaders with a vast playground to explore.

The park features more than 100 miles of trails specifically designed for dirt bikes, ATVs, and UTVs. These trails cater to offroading enthusiasts of all skill levels, from beginners looking for a thrilling adventure to experienced riders seeking new challenges. Visitors can experience the adrenaline rush of racing through the woods while taking in breathtaking mountain views.

If you're planning a trip to Hollerwood Park, make sure to check their website for detailed trail information, safety guidelines, and any park rules or restrictions. The park offers various amenities, including camping facilities and restroom facilities, to make your visit comfortable. Whether you're a solo rider, a group of friends, or a family looking for an exciting outdoor experience, Hollerwood Park is a must-visit offroad destination in Kentucky.

Key Features:

  • Over 43,000 acres of reclaimed coal lands
  • More than 100 miles of offroad trails
  • Suitable for dirt bikes, ATVs, and UTVs
  • Breathtaking mountain views
  • Camping facilities available

Black Mountain Off-Road Adventure Area

Black Mountain Off-Road Adventure Area is one of the most renowned offroad parks in Kentucky. Located in Evarts, it offers 8,000 acres of diverse terrain and over 150 miles of trails. The park was originally created from strip mining roads, providing a unique offroading experience with challenging trails for all skill levels. Visitors can enjoy the scenic views of the Appalachian Mountains while tackling the various trails. Additionally, the park features a canopy tour zip line and provides RV parks for overnight stays.

Land Between the Lakes National Recreation Area

The Land Between the Lakes National Recreation Area, situated between Kentucky Lake and Lake Barkley, is an expansive natural playground that offers miles of offroad trails for enthusiasts to explore. Covering more than 170,000 acres of land, this recreational area is a haven for outdoor enthusiasts seeking thrilling offroading adventures. While it is not a dedicated offroad park, it provides ample opportunities for offroaders to test their skills and enjoy the scenic beauty of the lakes and surrounding nature.

Every year, the Land Between the Lakes National Recreation Area hosts one of the largest Jeep Jamborees in the country, attracting offroaders from all around. This exciting event is a testament to the area's popularity among the offroading community.

Visitors can immerse themselves in offroading adventures while taking in the breathtaking views of the lakes and surrounding landscapes. The diverse terrain offers a variety of challenges for offroaders of all skill levels. Whether you prefer rocky climbs, muddy trails, or scenic drives, the Land Between the Lakes National Recreation Area has something to offer.

Exploring the Offroad Trails

With its vast expanse of land, the Land Between the Lakes National Recreation Area boasts a network of well-maintained offroad trails. These trails cater to a wide range of offroad vehicles, including ATVs, side-by-sides, and dirt bikes. Whether you're a seasoned offroader or a beginner, you'll find trails suited to your skill level.

As you navigate through the offroad trails, you'll experience the thrill of conquering various obstacles, including steep inclines, rocky terrains, and water crossings. The diverse landscapes offer a mix of challenges and scenic beauty, making each offroading adventure an unforgettable experience.

Planning Your Visit

Before heading to the Land Between the Lakes National Recreation Area, it's essential to plan your visit to make the most of your offroading experience. Here are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Check the weather conditions and trail status before your visit.
  • Make sure your offroad vehicle is in good working condition.
  • Pack necessary safety gear, including helmets, goggles, and protective clothing.
  • Respect the environment and stay on designated trails.
  • Follow all rules and regulations set by the recreation area.

By preparing adequately, you can ensure a safe and enjoyable offroading adventure at the Land Between the Lakes National Recreation Area.
