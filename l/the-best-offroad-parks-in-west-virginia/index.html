The Best Offroad Parks in West Virginia

01/31/2024

West Virginia is a haven for adventure enthusiasts, offering a plethora of off-road parks that cater to every level of off-roading expertise. Whether you're a seasoned pro or a beginner looking to embark on an adrenaline-fueled off-road adventure, West Virginia has something for everyone.

From the expansive trails of George Washington National Forest to the heart-pounding Hatfield-McCoy Trail System, off-road enthusiasts can explore thrilling terrains and breathtaking landscapes. With diverse options, West Virginia is an off-roading paradise waiting to be discovered.

Key Takeaways:

  • West Virginia offers a variety of off-road parks for adventure enthusiasts
  • George Washington National Forest provides over 130 miles of trails for all skill levels
  • The Hatfield-McCoy Trail System offers over 1,000 miles of off-roading fun
  • Burning Rock Off-Road Park in Beckley caters to all experience levels
  • New River ATV provides over 100 miles of trails in the scenic New River Gorge region

George Washington National Forest

Located in Virginia, George Washington National Forest is a haven for offroading enthusiasts. This expansive forest offers a wide array of offroading trails that cater to all skill levels, making it an ideal destination for beginners and experienced offroaders alike.

With over 130 miles of trails and an elevation gain of over 13,000 feet, George Washington National Forest provides an exhilarating offroading experience. Whether you prefer tackling steep inclines or navigating through rugged terrains, this park has something for everyone.

Explore the diverse landscapes of George Washington National Forest as you traverse through forests, valleys, and mountains. From breathtaking scenic views to challenging obstacles, every turn in the trail will leave you in awe of the natural beauty that surrounds you.

No matter if you're looking for a quick adventure or an all-day wheeling tour, George Washington National Forest offers a range of offroading options to suit your preferences. Take your time to conquer each trail and soak in the serenity of the forest, or challenge yourself to complete as many trails as you can in a single trip.

Whether you're a local or a visitor to Virginia, a trip to George Washington National Forest is a must for offroading enthusiasts. Get ready to embark on an unforgettable offroading journey and create memories that will last a lifetime.

Key Features of George Washington National Forest:

  • Over 130 miles of offroading trails
  • Elevation gain exceeding 13,000 feet
  • Diverse landscapes including forests, valleys, and mountains
  • Trails for all skill levels
  • Picturesque scenic views
Whether you're a beginner looking to hone your offroading skills or an experienced offroader seeking new thrills, George Washington National Forest has something for everyone. With its extensive trail network and stunning natural beauty, this forest is a playground for offroading adventures.

Hatfield-McCoy Trail System

The Hatfield-McCoy Trail System is a renowned offroad destination in West Virginia, offering a thrilling experience for offroading enthusiasts. With ten different trails to choose from, this trail system covers a vast expanse of over 1,000 miles, providing a diverse range of terrains and challenges.

Whether you're a beginner looking for scenic routes or a seasoned offroader seeking heart-pounding thrills, the Hatfield-McCoy Trail System has something for everyone. Take in the breathtaking beauty of the West Virginia landscape as you navigate through rugged mountains, lush forests, and scenic vistas.

Each trail within the Hatfield-McCoy system offers a unique offroading adventure. From the Hatfield-McCoy Rockhouse Trail, known for its challenging obstacles, to the Pinnacle Creek Trail, which boasts stunning views of the surrounding countryside, there's a trail suited to every skill level and preference.

For those seeking a true offroading expedition, the Hatfield-McCoy Trail System also offers the Outlaw Trail, an extensive 160-mile route that allows you to explore the rich history and heritage of the region while tackling challenging terrain.

Experience the thrill of offroading in West Virginia by immersing yourself in the Hatfield-McCoy Trail System. Whether you're riding a dirt bike, ATV, or UTV, you'll find adventure at every turn, surrounded by the natural beauty of the West Virginia wilderness.

Why Choose the Hatfield-McCoy Trail System?

  • Over 1,000 miles of offroad trails to explore
  • Varied terrain and difficulty levels for all skill levels
  • Breathtaking views and scenic vistas
  • Rich history and heritage along the Outlaw Trail
  • Opportunity to ride dirt bikes, ATVs, and UTVs

Burning Rock Off-Road Park

Burning Rock Off-Road Park, located near Beckley, is a 10,000-acre offroading paradise in West Virginia. With over 100 miles of diverse offroading trails, this park caters to enthusiasts of all experience levels, from beginners to seasoned pros. Whether you're looking for a leisurely ride or an adrenaline-pumping adventure, Burning Rock has something for everyone.

Explore the rugged beauty of West Virginia as you navigate through challenging terrains, including rocky hills, muddy bogs, and steep inclines. The park offers thrilling trails that will test your driving skills and provide an unforgettable offroading experience.

At Burning Rock Off-Road Park, you don't need to have your own vehicle to enjoy the adventure. The park provides rental equipment, including ATVs and UTVs, so you can easily hit the trails without any hassle. They have a well-maintained fleet of vehicles that are perfect for exploring the park's extensive network of trails.

Services and Amenities

  • Rental equipment available for a hassle-free adventure
  • Trails suitable for all experience levels
  • Diverse terrains including rocks, mud, and steep inclines
  • 10,000-acre park with over 100 miles of offroading trails
  • Well-maintained fleet of rental vehicles
  • On-site camping facilities for overnight stays

Whether you're a solo adventurer, a group of friends, or a family looking for an outdoor getaway, Burning Rock Off-Road Park is the perfect destination. Experience the thrill of offroading in the beautiful landscapes of West Virginia and create memories that will last a lifetime.

So gear up, rev your engines, and get ready for an incredible offroading adventure at Burning Rock Off-Road Park!

New River ATV

Located in the stunning New River Gorge region, New River ATV is a must-visit destination for offroading enthusiasts in West Virginia. With over 100 miles of thrilling trails, this outfitter offers unforgettable offroading adventures for both families and solo explorers.

Whether you're a beginner looking for a leisurely ride or an experienced offroader seeking heart-pounding challenges, New River ATV has a trail that suits your skill level. From serene woodland paths to rugged terrains, there's something for everyone.

At New River ATV, safety and enjoyment are top priorities. They provide well-maintained vehicles and necessary safety equipment to ensure a safe and exhilarating adventure. With their expert guides, you can explore the picturesque New River Gorge and its surrounding landscapes with confidence.

Trail Options

  • Beginner Trails: Perfect for novice offroaders, these well-marked trails offer a smooth and scenic ride through the breathtaking beauty of West Virginia's nature.
  • Intermediate Trails: Ready to step up your game? The intermediate trails at New River ATV provide a mix of challenges and scenic views, ideal for those looking to enhance their offroading skills.
  • Advanced Trails: For the adrenaline junkies and experienced offroaders, New River ATV offers a selection of advanced trails that will push your skills to the limit. Prepare for steep inclines, obstacles, and thrilling adventures.

No matter which trail you choose, you'll be immersed in the wild beauty of the New River Gorge. From dense forests to panoramic vistas, every moment on these trails is a chance to connect with nature and experience the thrill of offroading.

So, gear up and get ready for an offroading experience like no other with New River ATV. Unleash your adventurous spirit and conquer the offroad trails of New River Gorge in West Virginia.

Mountwood ATV Park

Located in the scenic Mid-Ohio Valley region, Mountwood ATV Park is a haven for offroading enthusiasts. With nearly 20 miles of thrilling offroading trails, this park offers an adrenaline-pumping experience for riders of all skill levels.

But Mountwood ATV Park isn't just about offroading; it also boasts over 50 miles of scenic hiking and biking trails, providing a perfect opportunity to explore the beauty of West Virginia's nature. Whether you're a seasoned hiker or a leisurely stroller, these trails promise unforgettable adventures.

This family-friendly park is a great destination for outdoor enthusiasts of all ages. Not only can you challenge yourself on the offroading trails, but you can also enjoy leisurely hikes or bike rides with your loved ones, surrounded by the picturesque landscapes of West Virginia.

So whether you're seeking the thrill of offroading or seeking peace in nature, Mountwood ATV Park has something for everyone. Come and experience the excitement of conquering the offroading trails or immerse yourself in the peaceful tranquility of the hiking and biking trails. You won't be disappointed.

Chaos Off-Road Park boasts a variety of trails that cater to different skill levels, allowing both novice and experienced offroaders to enjoy their time behind the wheel. From 4x4s to side by sides and most UTVs, Chaos Off-Road Park welcomes a range of vehicles, ensuring everyone can unleash their inner adventurer.
