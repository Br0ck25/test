Explore Thrills at Mountain Ridge ATV Park

01/31/2024

Pennsylvania is home to the thrilling Mountain Ridge ATV Park, a 3,000-acre off-roading paradise. With 150 miles of ATV trails, including a kids track, MX track, drag strip, and hill climbs, the park offers a wide range of terrain to challenge riders of all skill levels. In addition to the adrenaline-pumping trails, visitors can enjoy the scenic views and camping experience available at the park. Whether you're an ATV enthusiast or looking for an exciting outdoor adventure, Mountain Ridge ATV Park is the perfect destination.

Key Takeaways:

  • Mountain Ridge ATV Park in Pennsylvania offers thrilling off-roading adventure.
  • The park boasts 150 miles of ATV trails with various levels of difficulty.
  • Visitors can enjoy scenic views and a camping experience at the park.
  • Mountain Ridge ATV Park caters to riders of all skill levels, from beginners to experts.
  • Experience the adrenaline rush of off-roading and explore the great outdoors at Mountain Ridge ATV Park.

Unforgettable ATV Experiences in Pennsylvania

Pennsylvania is a haven for thrill-seekers looking for unforgettable ATV experiences. With its diverse terrain and stunning landscapes, the state offers something for every level of ATV enthusiast. Whether you're a beginner looking for a guided ATV tour or an experienced rider seeking a challenging off-road adventure, Pennsylvania has it all.

Explore Underground Trails at Mines & Meadows ATV/RV Resort

Mines & Meadows ATV/RV Resort is a unique destination where you can explore underground trails in an abandoned limestone mine. This one-of-a-kind experience provides an exhilarating off-road adventure unlike any other. The resort also offers 85 miles of above-ground ATV trails, allowing riders of all skill levels to enjoy the thrill of off-roading.

Conquer the Susquehannock ATV Trail

The Susquehannock ATV Trail is a must-visit for both beginners and experienced riders. With 43 miles of scenic trails winding through the picturesque Susquehannock State Forest, this trail offers breathtaking views and a memorable outdoor experience. Whether you prefer a leisurely ride or an adrenaline-pumping adventure, the Susquehannock ATV Trail has something for everyone.

Experience Spectacular Scenery at Rock Run Recreation Area

For those looking to combine off-roading with breathtaking views, the Rock Run Recreation Area is the perfect destination. With 140 miles of ATV trails spread across 6,000 acres, riders can enjoy the thrill of off-roading while taking in the spectacular scenery of the Allegheny Mountains. This ATV paradise offers an adrenaline rush and a visual feast for nature lovers.

Discover Over 100 Miles of ATV Trails in Allegheny National Forest

Allegheny National Forest is a haven for ATV enthusiasts, offering over 100 miles of designated trails. From beginner-friendly options like the Timberline Trail to more challenging routes like the Rocky Gap south loop, riders can explore a variety of terrains while surrounded by the beauty of the forest. This ATV trail network provides an opportunity to experience the great outdoors like never before.

ATV Parks and Resorts in Pennsylvania

In addition to Mountain Ridge ATV Park, Pennsylvania is home to several other exciting ATV parks and resorts. These destinations offer thrilling off-roading adventures and a wide range of amenities for ATV enthusiasts.

Mines & Meadows ATV/RV Resort

Mines & Meadows ATV/RV Resort is a unique destination that allows riders to explore both underground and above-ground trails. The resort features an abandoned limestone mine where riders can navigate through underground trails, providing an unforgettable adventure. In addition, the resort offers 85 miles of above-ground trails, catering to riders of all skill levels. From beginners to experienced riders, there's something for everyone at Mines & Meadows ATV/RV Resort.

Rock Run Recreation Area

Rock Run Recreation Area is another popular ATV park located in Pennsylvania. Spanning 6,000 acres, this expansive park offers 140 miles of diverse trails, promising an exhilarating off-roading experience. Riders can explore the park's scenic trails, which wind through mountains, forests, and fields. The stunning views and challenging terrain make Rock Run Recreation Area a favorite among ATV enthusiasts.

Anthracite Outdoor Adventure Area

If you're looking for even more riding fun, the Anthracite Outdoor Adventure Area should be on your list. With 8,000 acres of land, this vast area provides plenty of opportunities for outdoor recreation and off-roading adventure. The park features an extensive network of trails that cater to all skill levels, making it a great option for both beginners and experienced riders. From thrilling rides to scenic views, the Anthracite Outdoor Adventure Area offers an unforgettable ATV experience.

ATV Trails in Allegheny National Forest

Allegheny National Forest is a prime destination for ATV enthusiasts in Pennsylvania. With over 100 miles of designated ATV trails, riders can explore a variety of terrains and enjoy the beauty of the forest.

The Timberline Trail offers a beginner-friendly option, perfect for those looking to ease into ATV riding. This scenic trail winds through lush forests and offers a chance to spot wildlife along the way. It provides an excellent introduction to the thrill of off-roading in the Allegheny National Forest.

For more experienced riders seeking a challenge, the Rocky Gap south loop is a must-ride. This trail pushes the limits with its rugged terrain, tight curves, and steep inclines. When exploring the ATV trails in Allegheny National Forest, it is important to prioritize safety and follow all rules and regulations. By doing so, you can help preserve the forest's beauty and ensure a safe and enjoyable off-roading experience for all.

Plan Your Visit to Mountain Ridge ATV Park

If you're ready to experience the thrills of Mountain Ridge ATV Park, there are a few things to keep in mind. The park is open daily from 9 a.m. to 6 p.m., with riding rates ranging from $25 per person during the week to $30 per person on the weekends.

The park offers a variety of terrain, including 150 miles of trails and 40 miles of single track, ensuring that riders of all skill levels can find their perfect challenge. FAQ

What is the Mountain Ridge ATV Park?

Mountain Ridge ATV Park is a 3,000-acre off-roading paradise in Pennsylvania that offers 150 miles of ATV trails, including a kids track, MX track, drag strip, and hill climbs. It is a popular destination for outdoor recreation and adrenaline-pumping adventures.

What can I do at Mountain Ridge ATV Park?

At Mountain Ridge ATV Park, you can enjoy a variety of outdoor activities. You can explore the 150 miles of ATV trails, go mountain biking on designated trails, and even camp at the park. The park also offers scenic views and a camping experience amidst the beautiful surroundings.

Are there trails suitable for beginner riders at Mountain Ridge ATV Park?

Yes, Mountain Ridge ATV Park offers trails for riders of all levels, including beginners. There are designated trails that are suitable for those who are new to ATV riding, allowing them to have a safe and enjoyable experience.

Can I camp at Mountain Ridge ATV Park?

Yes, Mountain Ridge ATV Park provides campsites and rustic cabins for visitors who want to extend their stay. This allows you to fully immerse yourself in the outdoor beauty of Pennsylvania and enjoy a complete outdoor experience.

Are there guided ATV tours available at Mountain Ridge ATV Park?

Unfortunately, Mountain Ridge ATV Park does not offer guided ATV tours. However, you can still explore the park on your own and enjoy the thrills and excitement of off-roading.

What are some other ATV parks and resorts in Pennsylvania?

Pennsylvania is home to several other ATV parks and resorts. Some notable ones include Mines & Meadows ATV/RV Resort, Rock Run Recreation Area, and the Anthracite Outdoor Adventure Area. Each of these destinations offers a wide range of terrain and amenities for ATV enthusiasts.

Are there any scenic ATV trails in Pennsylvania?

Yes, Pennsylvania has several scenic ATV trails. Some popular ones include the Susquehannock ATV Trail, Whiskey Springs ATV Trail, Piney Rail Riders trail, and the ATV trails in Allegheny National Forest. These trails allow riders to enjoy the beauty of the Pennsylvania landscape while experiencing the thrill of off-roading.

Can children ride ATVs at Mountain Ridge ATV Park?

Yes, Mountain Ridge ATV Park offers a kids track that is suitable for children to ride ATVs. This allows families to enjoy the outdoor adventure together and create lasting memories.

How much does it cost to ride at Mountain Ridge ATV Park?

The cost of riding at Mountain Ridge ATV Park ranges from $25 per person during the week to $30 per person on the weekends. The park offers different rates depending on the day, allowing you to choose the best option based on your schedule.

What are the operating hours of Mountain Ridge ATV Park?

Mountain Ridge ATV Park is open daily from 9 a.m. to 6 p.m. This gives you ample time to enjoy the trails and outdoor activities offered at the park.

What should I bring when visiting Mountain Ridge ATV Park?

When visiting Mountain Ridge ATV Park, it is recommended to bring your own ATV, safety gear such as helmets and protective clothing, and any camping equipment if you plan on staying overnight. It is also important to bring your ID and proof of insurance for your ATV.
